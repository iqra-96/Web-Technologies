The Rabbit Meets a Wise Old Turtle

Learning the Value of Patience and Courage
And They Lived Happily Ever After

Once upon a time, there was a small rabbit who lived in a big green forest. Although he was tiny, he had very big dreams. He wanted to explore the world beyond the forest.

One day, the rabbit met a wise old turtle who taught him an important lesson. "Move forward step by step," said the turtle.
The rabbit learned that courage and patience make you stronger. This story teaches us to believe in ourselves.
